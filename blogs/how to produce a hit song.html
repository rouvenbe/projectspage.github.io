How to produce a hit song: Analysis of the determinants of chart success over the past decades

2022-03-27

This paper explores the key factors influencing chart success in the German music industry by analyzing over 200,000 songs using data from the Spotify API. By applying logistic regression models, the study identifies significant socioeconomic and musical determinants, such as audio features, timbre characteristics, and an artist's prior chart success. The best model predicts hit songs with 69% accuracy, highlighting that both musical optimization and artist popularity play critical roles in achieving top chart positions. The findings provide valuable insights for artists and producers seeking to increase their chances of charting hits.
