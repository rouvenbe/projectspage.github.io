The global financial cycle and debt capital fows to latin america: An ARDL bounds testing approach

2024-12-15

This study analyzes the dynamics of net debt capital flows in Brazil, Chile, and Mexico, focusing on both long-run and short-run relationships using an Error Correction Model (ECM). Using the ARDL bounds testing approach, I found evidence of a long-run relationship for Chile and Mexico, but not for Brazil. A negative long- run relationship is consistently observed between capital flows and key push factors, including the VIX, ex-post real policy interest rate, U.S. treasury yield, REER, and nominal interest rates.
