The global financial cycle and debt capital flows to latin america: An ARDL bounds testing approach

2024-12-15

This study analyzes the impact of the global financial cycle on net debt capital flows to Brazil, Chile, and Mexico, focusing on both long-run and short-run relationships using an error correction model (ECM). Using the ARDL bounds testing approach, the analysis finds evidence of a long-run relationship for Chile and Mexico, but not for Brazil.
